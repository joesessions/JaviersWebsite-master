A love for the Yamhill Valley About Our Company

Hello. I am Javier Montalvo. You killed my father. Prepare to die. Just kidding. My partner Nelson and I have lived all over North and South America. In 2015, we both left the Yamhill Valley for the last time. Quickly realizing we had left the most beautiful place on Earth, we combined our experience in the contruction fields and decided to start Javier's Painting in 2018. Newly formed, we are ecstatic with the opportunity to assist in the continual renewal and improvement of McMinnville and surrounding areas.
